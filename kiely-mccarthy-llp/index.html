Practice areas

Criminal law

One of the most experienced criminal defence practices in Limerick City. Our solicitors are recognised industry-wide as experts in serious criminal matters and are available 24 hours for Garda station advice.

  • Sexual offences and serious indictable matters
  • Drugs, firearms and money laundering
  • Road traffic offences and drink/drug driving

Civil litigation

Personal injury, defamation, judicial review and professional misconduct work for individual clients and businesses across Limerick and the Mid-West.

  • Personal injury claims*
  • Judicial review and unlawful detention

Regulatory law

We act for individuals and businesses facing inquiries from regulatory bodies — Health & Safety Authority, financial regulators, and professional disciplinary panels.

  • HSA prosecutions and inquiries
  • Regulatory and statutory offences
  • Professional disciplinary matters
  • Extradition proceedings
