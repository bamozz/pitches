Do I need a GP referral? +

No referral needed at all. You can book directly with us for physiotherapy or massage. If you're claiming through a care plan, we'll let you know what to bring.

How long is a first appointment? +

First appointments are always 45 minutes. This gives us enough time to hear your history, run a proper assessment and start building your treatment plan — not just hand you a printout at the door.

Do you accept Medicare or private health? +

Yes — we accept private health insurance (HICAPS on site) and Medicare Enhanced Primary Care plans. Not sure what you're covered for? Just ask us before you book.

What is the infrared sauna good for? +

Infrared sauna supports muscle recovery, reduces inflammation and improves circulation. Many patients book it between physio sessions to feel better faster. It's available as a standalone booking or as an add-on.

How quickly can I get an appointment? +

Usually within the same week. We deliberately keep our load manageable so you're never waiting three weeks to be seen — that's half the point.

Ready to feel better?

Same-week appointments are usually available. Book online, call us, or drop us a message — whichever is easiest for you.
