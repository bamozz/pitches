What we treat

If it hurts and stops you doing what you want, we should talk.

Back & neck pain

Acute strains, persistent pain, posture-related discomfort. McKenzie-trained therapists for spinal cases.

Sports injuries

Hamstring strains, ankle sprains, tennis elbow, runner's knee. Return-to-sport rehab plans.

Post-op rehab

Knee replacements, ACL reconstructions, rotator cuff repairs. We work with your surgeon's protocol.

Hand & wrist

Specialist hand physiotherapy — splinting, post-fracture rehab, repetitive strain.

Pilates & rehab classes

Small-group reformer and mat Pilates, plus dedicated back classes for ongoing recovery.

Headaches & TMJ

Tension headaches, migraine triggers, jaw pain. Manual therapy plus home exercises.

Children & teens

Growing-pain assessments, scoliosis screening, sports rehab for school-age athletes.

Lymphatic drainage

Manual lymphatic drainage post-cancer surgery and for chronic oedema management.

The team

Led by Theresa McGinn, a chartered physio for over 30 years.

Theresa founded the clinic in 1994. Since then the team has grown to eight therapists, each with a specialism — hand therapy, McKenzie spinal work, Pilates instruction, athletic therapy and lymphatic drainage among them. You will see the same therapist for follow-up sessions wherever possible.

  • Chartered Physiotherapists, ISCP-registeredEvery clinician on the team is registered with the Irish Society of Chartered Physiotherapists.
  • Direct access — no GP referral neededYou can book straight in. Insurance providers including VHI, Laya and Irish Life are accepted.
  • One therapist throughout your treatmentContinuity matters. We aim to keep you with the same physio across your full plan.
