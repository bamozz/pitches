What happens in a physiotherapy session? +

Treatment typically combines hands-on therapy including joint mobilisation, dry needling, soft tissue work and stretching, with a personalised home exercise program delivered via cloud based software so you can follow it anywhere.

Who will I be seeing? +

Roshan Singh (BSc Physio, APAM) is the owner and practice principal with over 10 years at the clinic. Toni Kirk (BPHEd, BSc Physio, APAM) brings 12 years as a physical education teacher before moving into physio. Carol Canzirri is a remedial massage therapist with 18 years of practice in Perth.

What types of massage do you offer? +

Carol offers remedial massage using trigger point therapy, myofascial release, sports massage and passive stretching. She also specialises in pregnancy massage, scar tissue work and craniosacral release.

Is the infrared sauna available to me? +

The sauna is exclusively available to existing patients. Each booking is private, with 40 minutes of exclusive use of the 3 person room. Towels and refreshments are provided and the sauna is pre-heated on arrival.

What are your opening hours? +

Monday to Thursday 8 a.m. to 7 p.m., Friday 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. You can book online or call us on 9444 9663.
