How it works

Three sessions to know whether physio is the answer.

If we can't show you a measurable improvement in three sessions, we'll tell you straight — and refer you on if your case needs imaging, a surgeon or a different specialist. We are not the right answer for everyone, and we'd rather you knew that early.

  1. First session: assessment & treatment60-minute first appointment. We diagnose what's actually causing the pain — not just where it hurts — and start treatment the same day.
  2. Second & third: hands-on plus homeworkManual therapy in clinic, plus a prescribed exercise plan you actually follow because it takes 10 minutes a day, not 45.
  3. Discharge when you're betterWe don't book you in for sessions you don't need. Once you're moving freely and pain-free, we discharge with a maintenance plan.
