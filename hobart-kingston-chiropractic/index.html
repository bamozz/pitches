Do I need a referral to see a chiropractor? +

No. You can book directly without a GP referral. If you have relevant scans or reports from other practitioners, bring them along — they're helpful, but not required.

How many sessions will I need? +

It depends on your condition, how long you've had it and your overall health. We give you an honest estimate at your first appointment. Most acute issues resolve in 4–8 sessions; longer-standing problems take more time.

Is the adjustment painful? +

A chiropractic adjustment is not painful for most people. You may feel a brief sensation and hear a pop (gas releasing from the joint), often followed by immediate relief. We always explain what we're doing and check your comfort throughout.

Do you treat children and older adults? +

Yes. We regularly treat patients from teenagers through to their eighties. Techniques are adapted to suit age, size and condition. For children under 12, a parent or guardian is always present in the room.

What should I wear or bring? +

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is best. Bring any relevant X-rays, MRI reports or referral letters you have. For your first visit, arrive five minutes early to complete a brief health history form.
